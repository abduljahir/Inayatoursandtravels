Armenia's top tourist attraction is the Geghard Monastery, a UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its unique rock-cut architecture and historical significance. Founded in the 4th century, parts of the monastery are carved directly into the surrounding cliffs of the Azat River gorge.
